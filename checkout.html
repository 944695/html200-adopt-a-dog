About Lucas

Lucas is a brilliant, high-energy Border Collie mix who arrived at our shelter after being found as a stray. Despite his rough start, he is one of the sweetest, most eager-to-please dogs we've ever had.

He knows basic commands like sit, stay, and shake — and he picks up new skills in minutes. Lucas thrives with an active family that can give him the exercise and mental stimulation he craves. He does best as the only pet in the home, but loves all people, including children.

Temperament

  • Energetic & playful
  • Highly intelligent
  • Loyal & affectionate
  • Good with children
  • Leash-trained
